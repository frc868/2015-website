About Team 868

Our Story

The Carmel High School TechHOUNDS, FRC Team 868, is a high school robotics team that participates in the international FIRST Robotics Competition. TechHOUNDS was founded thirteen years ago by Carmel High School architecture teacher Mr. David Smith. Since 2001, TechHOUNDS has grown from 20 students to a whopping 92 committed young adults. While we are a student driven team, Team 868 is lucky to be mentored by 38 professional engineers, parents, teachers, and alumni who foster technical and professional skills.We the Carmel High School Techhounds, FRC team 868, are a robotics team
